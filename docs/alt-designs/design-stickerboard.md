## Design Direction

Community Stickerboard

Vibe: Playful • Social • Handmade

Palette:

- #5B8DEF — Friendly blue (primary UI)
- #F97316 — Bright orange (primary actions)
- #10B981 — Community green (success / “looking for members”)
- #1F2933 — Deep charcoal (text / structure)
- #F6F5F3 — Soft off-white (background)

Fonts: Manrope (headlines) + Source Sans 3 (body)
Inspiration: Discord

Feels like a welcoming community hub. Soft shadows, rounded avatars, colored “community chips,” and simple card layouts
that emphasize people and teams. Slightly imperfect shapes, sticker-like tags for roles (“coach,” “team,” “opener”).

## Visual Description

The landing page feels like a lively community board inside a bright digital clubhouse. A wide, airy hero sits at the
top with a soft off-white background and scattered playful shapes—rounded blobs and sticker-like labels that feel
slightly imperfect. The headline is big, friendly, and confident, set in a rounded sans-serif that feels social and
welcoming.

Below it, colorful “sticker tags” float around the hero: Find a Team, Find a Coach, Join a Practice Group—each in
slightly irregular pill shapes with bright accents like coral, teal, and warm orange.

The layout is clean and grid-based, but loosened with playful overlaps. Cards for teams, coaches, and openers sit in
roomy rows with generous spacing and soft shadows. Each card has a small avatar cluster, a bold title, and a bright tag
badge (“Looking for Members,” “Coach Available”).

Buttons are chunky rounded pills with solid colors and subtle hover bounce.

The whole page feels like a friendly improv community hub—organized, but full of personality and human energy.
